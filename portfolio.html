How It Works

My Process

01
Discovery Call
We discuss your vision, goals, timeline, and deliverables. I ask the right questions to understand exactly what story you want to tell.
02
Asset Review
I review all footage, audio, and reference materials. An edit brief is prepared with structure, pacing, and aesthetic direction mapped out.
03
First Assembly
A rough cut is delivered for feedback. Story structure and pacing locked before fine editing begins.
04
Fine Cut & Color
Precision editing, motion graphics, color grading, and sound design all applied. Up to three revision rounds included.
05
Final Delivery
Export in all required formats and specs — broadcast, web, social. Full project archive delivered for your records.
